PJ's Movie List

The Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Lord of the Rings (2001)

An absolute banger of a movie. Extraordinary casting and music.

The Matrix (1999)

S-tier fight choreography and featuring one of my favorite actors -- Hugo Weaving

Airplane! (1980)

Featuring the god of comedy, Leslie Neilson. The jokes never stop and there are a multitude of quotable moments.
