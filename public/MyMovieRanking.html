The Best Movies of All Time

My Top 3 Movies

The Lord of the Rings

A bit of a cheat as it is a trilogy. This fantasy epic is exceptional in every way, and no matter how many times I watch it, I will never tire of it.

Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon

Excellent fight sequences, and Tan Dun's music adds incredible ambience.

Cloud Atlas

Several seemingly different storylines become interconnected in a very interesting way.
